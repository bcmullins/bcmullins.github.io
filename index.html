About Me

I am a researcher and graduate student at UMass Amherst working with Dan Sheldon and Gerome Miklau as well as a Visiting Professor in Computer Science at Mount Holyoke College.

Before this, I was a Data Scientist at Allstate and a researcher at the Fiscal Research Center, a public policy think tank in Atlanta. I studied mathematics & logic at Miami University and philosophy & economics at Georgia State University.
